## How To Use The App
### Register
To register a new user, click on the `Register` button on the top right of the page. This will take you to the register page where you can enter your details. Once you have entered your details, click on the `Register` button to register your account.
### Login
To login, click on the `Login` button on the top right of the page. This will take you to the login page where you can enter your details. Once you have entered your details, click on the `Login` button to login to your account.
### Logout
To logout, click on the `Logout` button on the top right of the page. This will log you out of your account and take you back to the home page.
### Account
To view your account details, click on your account name's button on the top right of the page. This will take you to the account page where you can view your details.
### Home
To go back to the home page, click on the `Home` button on the top left of the page. This will take you back to the home page.
### Customers
To view the customers, click on the `Customers` button on the top left of the page. This will take you to the customers page where you can view the customers.
##### Create New
To create a new customer, click on the `Create New` button on the top left of the page. This will take you to the create new customer page where you can enter the customer's details. Once you have entered the customer's details, click on the `Create` button to create the customer.
##### Edit
To edit a customer, click on the `Edit` button on the right of a customer's row. This will take you to the edit customer page where you can edit the customer's details. Once you have edited the customer's details, click on the `Save` button to edit the customer.
##### Details
To view a customer's details, click on the `Details` button on the right of a customer's row. This will take you to the details page where you can view the customer's details.
##### Delete
To delete a customer, click on the `Delete` button on the right of a customer's row. This will delete the customer.
### Orders

##### Create New

##### Details

##### Delete
To delete an order, click on the `Delete` button on the right of an order's row. This will delete the order.
### Products

##### Create New
